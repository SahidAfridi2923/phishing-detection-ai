1️⃣ What is Phishing?

Answer:
Phishing is a cyber attack where hackers create fake websites that look like real websites to steal user information like passwords, bank details, or personal data.

Example: Fake login pages that look like Google or PayPal.



2️⃣ What is the Aim of Your Project?

Answer:
The aim of this project is to detect whether a website URL is safe or phishing and warn the user before they open the fake website.



3️⃣ Why is Phishing Dangerous?

Answer:
Phishing can steal user passwords, money, and personal information. It can also cause financial loss and identity theft.
